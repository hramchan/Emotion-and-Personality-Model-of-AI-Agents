Conclusions:

Conclusion: The intent for this paper to create content theory of emotion, particularly observing emotion of “love”. This was achieved using AI technique, rule-based system to achieve ideas of emotion based on previous emotions and how intelligents perceive their world and other intelligent agents.

Summary: My intent for this paper was to evaluate personalities based on personal stories, events, learning experience from other agents to construct a content theory of “love”, an emotion intrinsic in nature yet complex to understand.  There are three main intelligent agents observed in the domain, “parent”, “child”, and “God” all which have their own goals, standards, and preferences (GSP). Depending on the personality of agent, each agent perceives its world in terms of love, thus elicits an emotion according to their own GSP. The emotions elicited are created by implementation of AI technique, constructing a rule based system.
